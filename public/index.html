Keyboards with a mind of their own

We make affordable, bespoke, one-of-a-kind mechanical keyboards; packed with features. We aim to dramatically expand what your keyboard can be and what it can do.

features

Your Unique Layoutkeyboard

Arrange up to 200 reprogrammable keys in any pattern you like. Optomize your layout for your workflow weather that's editing video, streaming your favorite game, or just how you like to type.

Zero-install reprogrammingphonelink_setup

Use any WiFi enabled device to securely reprogram your keyboard without installing or flashing anything. Simply connect to your keyboard's wifi and open a browser.

WiFiwifi

Use WiFi connectivity to reconfigure your keyboard from your phone, download firmware updates, trigger a home automation, access your favorite web API....and anything else you can imagine!

BlueToothbluetooth

Connect to a second device via BlueTooth. You can even switch between the devices with the press of a key once paired.

Addressable RGBlight_mode

Make your board stand out with dazzling full-color lighting. Customize each individual light to change color in response to your keypresses, API calls, or your set pattern. Don't want to bother getting lost in the details? Use one of our ready made patterns.

Rotary Encodersflip_camera_android

Weather you want a rotary encoder with detents, or without, or several of both; we've got you covered. All encoders have a push switch and have capacitive touch capabilities.

Did we miss something?feedback

We're always adding new capabilities to our keyboard's hardware and software. Let us know what features you want in your keyboard.
